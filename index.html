About Our School

Sulop National High School is a public secondary educational institution committed to providing quality education to the youth of Sulop and neighboring communities.

School Information

School Type: Public Secondary School under DepEd

School ID: 304297

Established: 1966

School Head: Jerome G. Samson

Mission & Vision

Vision: Sulop National High School produces quality education in a culture of excellence demonstrated by the strength of competent teachers, cooperative parents, world-class instructional leaders with adequate resources that provide viability of purpose in a nurturing environment.

Mission: The school exists to provide students with functional curriculum, adequate resources, quality instructions by the competent and dedicated teachers and establishes a strong partnership with the community to produce higher learning outcome.

Core Values: Maka-Diyos, Makatao, Makakalikasan, Makabansa

School Location

Complete Address: CII Drive, Poblacion, Sulop, Davao del Sur, Philippines

How to get to Sulop National High School:

  • From Davao City: Take a bus bound for Digos City and alight at Sulop terminal
  • From Digos City: Take a bus or van going to Sulop (approximately 1 hour travel time)
  • Within Sulop: From the town proper, take a tricycle to the school (5-10 minute ride)
  • Private Vehicle: Ample parking space is available within the school compound

Coordinates: 6.6072273° N, 125.3436386° E

Academic Programs

Sulop National High School offers a comprehensive secondary education program aligned with the K-12 curriculum of the Department of Education.

Grades 7 to 10 following the enhanced basic education curriculum.

  • Grade 8
