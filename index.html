About Our Senior High School

Sulop National High School Senior High Department is committed to providing quality senior high education to the youth of Sulop and neighboring communities, preparing students for higher education, employment, or entrepreneurship.

Senior High Information

School Type: Public Senior High School under DepEd

School ID: 304297

SHS Established: 2016

Grade Levels: Grades 11 & 12

Mission & Vision

Vision: Sulop National High School Senior High produces quality education in a culture of excellence demonstrated by the strength of competent teachers, cooperative parents, world-class instructional leaders with adequate resources that provide viability of purpose in a nurturing environment.

Mission: The Senior High Department exists to provide students with functional curriculum, adequate resources, quality instructions by competent and dedicated teachers and establishes a strong partnership with the community to produce higher learning outcomes for college, career, and life readiness.

Core Values: Maka-Diyos, Makatao, Makakalikasan, Makabansa

School Location

Complete Address: CII Drive, Poblacion, Sulop, Davao del Sur, Philippines

How to get to Sulop National High School Senior High:

  • From Davao City: Take a bus bound for Digos City and alight at Sulop terminal
  • From Digos City: Take a bus or van going to Sulop (approximately 1 hour travel time)
  • Within Sulop: From the town proper, take a tricycle to the school (5-10 minute ride)
  • Private Vehicle: Ample parking space is available within the school compound

Coordinates: 6.6072273° N, 125.3436386° E

Enrollment Information

For incoming Grade 11 students and transferees, please prepare the following requirements:

  • Original Form 138 (Report Card) from Junior High
  • PSA/NSO Birth Certificate (photocopy and original for verification)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 ID pictures (3 copies)
  • Long brown envelope

Enrollment Schedule: April to May (for incoming Grade 11), June (for late enrollees)
