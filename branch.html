School Principal

Meet our dedicated school principal who leads Sulop National High School with vision and excellence.

Jerome G. Samson, MACE

School Principal I

Mr. Jerome G. Samson serves as the principal of Sulop National High School, overseeing the overall administration and educational excellence of both Junior and Senior High School departments. With years of experience in education leadership, he is committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment for all students.

Principal's Biography

Educational Journey & Leadership

Mr. Jerome G. Samson has been serving as an educational leader for over 15 years. He holds a Master's Degree in Educational Management and a Bachelor's Degree in Secondary Education. His journey in education began as a Mathematics teacher, where he developed innovative teaching strategies that improved student performance.

Throughout his career, Mr. Samson has received numerous awards for educational leadership and has been instrumental in implementing curriculum reforms that align with DepEd's K-12 program. He believes in creating a school environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Under his leadership, Sulop National High School has seen significant improvements in student achievement, teacher development, and community engagement.
